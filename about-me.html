ABOUT ME

Hi there! I'm Henriette Huus, an aspiring UX designer currently residing in Oslo. I'm passionate about crafting meaningful user experiences that positively impact people's lives. Currently, I'm enrolled in a 2-year vocational school program in UX design at the Vocational School of Kristiania, honing my skills and expertise in this field.


Additionally, I've completed a 6-month course on UX design offered by Google through Coursera, further expanding my knowledge and understanding of user-centered design principles.


When I'm not immersed in design and research, you can find me indulging in my hobbies. I love knitting, diving into captivating books, immersing myself in video games, and spending quality time with friends.


Feel free to reach out to me or connect with me on LinkedIn! I'm always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and conversations within the UX design community.
